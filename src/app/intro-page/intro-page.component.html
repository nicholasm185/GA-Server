Welcome to our TRIP PLANNER RECOMMENDATION

Thanks for your willingness to participate in this study.

In this experiment, we ask you to evaluate our trip planner recommendation, by following the step below:
  1. Specify your constraint: budget, days (trip duration), number of persons, then choose preferred activity
  2. In this step, you may or may not tell the system what is your preferred activities.
  3. You will see the pairs of options. Every time you see the pair, you only need to choose one option which you think better than the other in a pair. You may skip if you think none of the choice matches your preference. You may go back and revise your preferences.
  4. If you are happy with your choice, you can submit and let the machine calculate the best trip for you. There are two trips calculated:
    • Type A
    • Type B
    Please evaluate those two types
  5. After done with those two, you need to fill in the questionnaire at the end of the pages.
